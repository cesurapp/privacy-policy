N Translator — Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the N Translator mobile application developed by Cesur Apaydın.

Information Collection and Use

The N Translator app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. You can use the app without registration or login. Microphone access is used solely for real-time voice translation; no recordings are stored or transmitted.

Location Data

The application does not collect or use precise location data from your device.

Third-Party Access

Because the app does not collect any data, no information is shared with third parties.

Opt-Out Rights

You may stop all use of the app at any time by uninstalling it from your device using the standard uninstall process.

Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided information, please contact us at apaydin541@gmail.com so we can take appropriate action.

Security

Since no data is collected or stored, there is no risk of unauthorized access to user information.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Updates will be posted on this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
