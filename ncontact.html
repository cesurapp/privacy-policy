Quick help and contact information for the N Translator mobile app.

About N Translator

N Translator lets you translate text and speech quickly, easily, and accurately. With its clean interface, you can type or speak to get instant translations. Microphone access is used only for voice translation — your recordings are never stored or shared.

Key Features

  • Fast text translation: type, paste, or copy text instantly.
  • Voice translation: speak and get instant results.
  • Share and copy: easily copy or share translations.
  • Simple, intuitive design: ideal for travel, business, or everyday use.
